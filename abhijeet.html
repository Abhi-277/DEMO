Virat Kohli IPL 2016 Statistics

Virat Kohli has represented RCB in Indian Premier League 2016. Virat Kohli showed great performance in that edition & scored 973 runs in 16 matches. 2016 IPL is most important for him because he scored 4 centuries & seven fifties in that edition. In almost every game, he scored valuable runs to achieve the required total. He struck 83 fours & 38 sixes. His highest score was 113 runs. In contrast to all the previous editions of IPL, he presented extraordinary performance. As far as bowling is concern, he only bowl one over in which he conceded 13 runs.

Virat kohli is the fastest player to achieve 25 centuries milestone. That is a big life achievement for him.

Kohli remained a vital player of T-20 game. He remained Man of the match 9 times resulting all games in victory for his team.
